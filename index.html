The 5 ATS Mistakes

Why your CV is being binned
before anyone reads it

None of these are about your experience. All of them are fixable today.

01

The Invisible Table Trap

Tables and columns look professional to humans. To an ATS they're scrambled data, merging your job title with your hobbies and scoring you zero.

Formatting
02

The Header Ghost

Your name and contact details in the document header are invisible to many UK ATS systems, including NHS portals. The bot can't find your number to invite you for interview.

Contact Info
03

The Keyword Mismatch

"Liaising with clients" and "Stakeholder Management" mean the same thing to you. To the ATS, only one scores a point. The algorithm is scanning for exact phrases from the job description.

Keywords
04

The Font Problem

Custom fonts from Canva or Google Fonts render as empty boxes or gibberish if the ATS doesn't have them installed. Your entire CV becomes unreadable.

Design
05

The PDF vs DOCX Debate

PDF is not always the right answer. Many older UK recruitment portals, especially in the public sector, parse .docx more accurately. One format doesn't fit all.

File Format
